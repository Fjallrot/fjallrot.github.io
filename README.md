> **Fjallrót (Faroese for "Mountain Root")** symbolizes stability, resilience, and safety. Based in Tórshavn, Faroe Islands, I design and build privacy-centric, native mobile applications tailored for Faroese citizens and the wider world.

---

## ⛰️ Our Philosophy

I believe software should help people in their daily lives without compromising their autonomy. 
* **Privacy First (Dataloyvi):** No tracking, no ads, no telemetry, and no third-party libraries collecting personal data. What happens on your device stays on your device.
* **Offline-First (Uttan Net):** Designed for the Faroese terrain. Our apps work fully offline, whether you are in a remote valley, on a ferry, or inside a mountain tunnel.
* **Local Relevance:** Built with native Faroese translations and localized content to preserve culinary heritage and guide citizens in local legislation.
* **Clean Engineering:** Developed natively using modern Android standards (Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, SQLite, and Room) to ensure zero battery drain and smooth performance.

---

## 📱 Our Apps

### ⚖️ Rættarvísi (Faroese Law Guide)
A comprehensive legal information guide designed for Faroese citizens.
* **Statutes & Civil Rights:** Easily browse and search law codes offline.
* **Plain Language Guidance:** Explanations of common citizen rights and welfare options.
* **Privacy Assured:** Your search history and clicked statutes are never recorded or uploaded.

### 🍲 Recipe App (Føroyskur Matur)
Preserving and celebrating the culinary heritage of the Faroe Islands.
* **Authentic Local Recipes:** Traditional methods for preparing *skerpikjøt*, *ræstur fiskur*, rhubarb pies, and more.
* **Portion Calculator:** Adjust ingredient scaling based on the number of dinner guests.
* **Hjallur Guides:** Practical tips on traditional air-drying temperatures and drying house conditions.

## 💻 Tech Stack

* **Language:** Kotlin
* **UI Framework:** Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
* **Local Database:** SQLite / Room Database
* **On-Device Intelligence:** TensorFlow Lite (running local privacy-focused AI models)

---

### Features
* **Nordic Design System:** A clean midnight-blue visual aesthetic representing the Faroese environment, utilizing CSS custom properties, responsive grids, and subtle micro-animations.
* **Bilingual Localization:** Seamless English (`EN`) and Faroese (`FO`) language toggles.
* **Interactive Device Mockup:** A simulated Android smartphone viewport built in pure HTML/CSS/JS. Users can select apps and interact with functional screens (e.g. search laws or view recipe detail sheets) live on the site.
